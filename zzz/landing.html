What is FinSync?

FinSync is a complete personal finance management platform where you can track income and expenses, set budgets, manage savings goals, split bills on trips, and manage shared group finances — all from one clean dashboard.

Can I split expenses with friends?

Yes! FinSync has a built-in Trips & Splits module. Create a trip, add expenses, choose equal or custom splits, and track who owes whom. You can mark settlements as done when payments are made.

Can I create groups for family expenses?

Absolutely. You can create Family or Flatmate groups, invite members, and view a combined expense dashboard to understand collective spending patterns.

Can I download my financial reports?

Yes. FinSync lets you generate monthly or custom-range reports and export them as PDF or Excel files — useful for accountants, tax filing, or personal record-keeping.

Is my financial data secure?

Your data is encrypted and stored securely. FinSync uses role-based access control so users only see their own data. We never sell or share your information with third parties.
